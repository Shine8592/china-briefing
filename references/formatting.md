# Formatting Guidance for 嘟嘟点评

- **Purpose**: Ensure the AI commentary (嘟嘟点评) is unmistakably visible and clearly separated from the factual news content.
- **Style**:
  - Prefix each commentary paragraph with the bold label `**嘟嘟点评:**`
  - Keep the commentary length between **50‑100** characters
  - Avoid mixing the label and the commentary on the same line without a clear separator
- **Example**:
  ```markdown
  **头条1** [事实 100‑200字] …
  **嘟嘟点评:** 这意味着……（50‑100字）
  ```
- **What makes a good 嘟嘟点评**:
  - ✅ 加背景："XX做XXX，结合之前的YYY，说明ZZZ"
  - ✅ 给观点："这意味着…我看好/担心…"
  - ✅ 连点："这和之前的XX配对，说明趋势在…"
  - ✅ 对读者："对我们来说，意味着…"
  - ❌ 只复述："XX报道了XXX"
